1. Who We Are

Casa da Lili is a wellness retreat offering short-term stays and holistic therapy sessions, located in Portugal. The data controller for personal data collected through this website is Lili, reachable at casadalili.bemestar@gmail.com or by phone at +351 966 789 248.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your message

We do not collect payment card details or sensitive health data through this website.

3. Why We Collect It

Your data is used solely to respond to your enquiry, provide information about our programmes and therapies, and process booking requests. We do not use it for marketing without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

Processing is based on Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR — necessary for taking steps at your request prior to entering a contract — and, where applicable, Article 6(1)(a) — your consent when you voluntarily submit the form.

5. Analytics

This website uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly analytics tool. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not track individual visitors across sessions, and does not collect personally identifiable information. Only aggregate page-view counts are stored.

6. Data Retention

We retain enquiry messages for as long as necessary to manage the booking relationship, and for up to 12 months thereafter. You may request deletion at any time (see Your Rights below).

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law or where strictly necessary to operate our services (e.g. our email provider). All processors are bound by appropriate data protection agreements.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at casadalili.bemestar@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Right to Complain

If you believe we have handled your data unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Portuguese data protection authority, the CNPD (Comissão Nacional de Proteção de Dados) at cnpd.pt.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of this website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
